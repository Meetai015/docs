# Getting Started with Gistly

Welcome to Gistly! This guide will help you set up your account and start analyzing your first meetings.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Gistly account (sign up at [console.gistly.ai](https://console.gistly.ai))
- Admin access to connect your meeting platform (Microsoft Teams, Zoom, etc.)

## Step 1: Create Your Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [console.gistly.ai](https://console.gistly.ai) and create your account
    using your work email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Workspace">
    Create a workspace for your team. You can invite team members later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Add your name and role to personalize your experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Connect Your Meeting Platform

Gistly integrates with popular meeting platforms to automatically ingest recordings.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Microsoft Teams">
    1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Integrations** 2. Click **Connect Microsoft
    Teams** 3. Sign in with your Microsoft admin account 4. Grant the required
    permissions 5. Select which users to sync
    <Note>
      Connecting Teams requires Azure AD admin consent. See our [Teams Setup
      Guide](/guides/connecting-teams) for details.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Zoom">
    1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Integrations** 2. Click **Connect Zoom** 3.
    Sign in with your Zoom account 4. Authorize Gistly access
    <Info>
      Zoom integration is coming soon. Join the waitlist to be notified.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Manual Upload">
    Don't use Teams or Zoom? You can upload recordings manually: 1. Go to
    **Meetings** → **Upload** 2. Drag and drop your audio/video files 3.
    Processing starts automatically
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Process Your First Meeting

Once connected, recordings will be automatically processed. You can also upload manually:

1. Navigate to **Meetings** → **Upload**
2. Select your audio or video file
3. Wait for processing (typically 2-5 minutes)
4. View your transcript and insights

## Step 4: Explore Insights

After processing, you'll have access to:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Transcript" icon="file-lines">
    Full meeting transcript with speaker labels and timestamps
  </Card>
  <Card title="Summary" icon="sparkles">
    AI-generated summary with key points and action items
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analytics" icon="chart-bar">
    Talk time, participation metrics, and more
  </Card>
  <Card title="Share" icon="share">
    Share insights with your team or export to other tools
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Step 5: Invite Your Team

Collaborate with your team by inviting them to your workspace:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter email addresses
4. Set their role (Admin, Member, or Viewer)
