## When Contacting Support

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

- **Account email** - The email you use to log in
- **Description** - Clear explanation of the issue
- **Steps to reproduce** - What were you doing when the issue occurred?
- **Screenshots** - If applicable
- **Meeting ID** - For issues with specific recordings

## Feature Requests

Have an idea for Gistly? We'd love to hear from you!

Email **product@gistly.ai** with your feature request. Please include:

- What problem the feature would solve
- How you envision it working
- How important it is to your workflow

## Bug Reports

Found a bug? Help us fix it by reporting to **notes@gistly.ai** with:

- Steps to reproduce the issue
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Browser and device information
- Screenshots or screen recordings
