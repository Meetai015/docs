# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Gistly.

## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Gistly?" icon="lightbulb">
    Gistly is an AI-powered meeting analytics platform that transforms your
    meeting recordings into transcripts, summaries, and actionable insights.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Gistly work?" icon="gears">
    1. Connect your meeting platform (Teams, Zoom, etc.) or upload recordings 2.
    Gistly processes recordings using advanced AI 3. Get transcripts, summaries,
    and analytics within minutes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which meeting platforms are supported?" icon="plug">
    Currently supported: - Microsoft Teams (full integration) - Manual upload
    (any audio/video format) Coming soon: - Zoom - Google Meet - Webex
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?" icon="shield-halved">
    Absolutely. We use: - AES-256 encryption at rest - TLS 1.3 encryption in
    transit - SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure (in progress) - Regular
    security audits
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where is my data stored?" icon="database">
    Data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure. Enterprise customers can
    choose their data residency region (US, EU, Asia-Pacific).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can see my recordings?" icon="eye">
    Only users in your workspace with appropriate permissions can access
    recordings. Workspace admins control access levels.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data used to train AI models?" icon="robot">
    No. Your data is never used to train our AI models. We use your data solely
    to provide you with transcription and analytics services.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How accurate is the transcription?" icon="bullseye">
    Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy for clear audio with native English speakers.
    Accuracy may vary for: - Heavy accents - Poor audio quality - Multiple
    overlapping speakers - Technical jargon (can be improved with custom
    vocabulary)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages are supported?" icon="language">
    Currently we support English with the highest accuracy. Additional languages
    coming soon: - Spanish - French - German - Portuguese - Hindi
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit transcripts?" icon="pen">
    Yes! You can edit transcripts directly in the Gistly dashboard. Edits are
    saved and reflected in all exports.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does processing take?" icon="clock">
    Processing typically takes 2-5 minutes for a 1-hour recording. Longer
    recordings may take proportionally more time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Is there an API?" icon="code">

</AccordionGroup>
