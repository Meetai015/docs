# Features

Discover everything Gistly can do to help your sales, support, and collections teams perform at their best.

## Conversation Intelligence

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Conversation QA & Risk Intelligence" icon="shield-check">
    Automate quality assurance across 100% of your conversations:
    - Score every call against customizable templates
    - Detect compliance violations and red flags automatically
    - Track SOP adherence across teams and agents
    - Get instant alerts on high-risk conversations
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Transcription" icon="file-lines">
    High-accuracy transcription in 100+ languages:
    - Multiple speaker identification with diarization
    - Automatic timestamps and speaker labels
    - Support for technical terminology and accents
    - Audio and video file support (MP3, WAV, MP4, and more)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI Summaries & Action Items" icon="sparkles">
    Get concise, actionable meeting summaries:
    - Key discussion points and decisions
    - Action items with assignees
    - Follow-up requirements
    - Customer sentiment and intent analysis
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Analytics & Reporting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Performance Dashboards" icon="chart-line">
    Track key metrics across your team:
    - Call quality scores and trends
    - Agent performance comparisons
    - Talk time distribution and participation rates
    - Custom date ranges and filters
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom Scoring Templates" icon="sliders">
    Build scoring templates tailored to your business:
    - Opening, objection handling, closing criteria
    - Compliance and regulatory checkpoints
    - Custom parameters with weighted scoring
    - Template versioning and A/B testing
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reports & Exports" icon="file-chart-pie">
    Generate and share reports:
    - Agent-level and team-level reports
    - Trend analysis over time
    - Export to CSV for further analysis
    - Scheduled report delivery
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security & Compliance

<Info>
  Your data security is our top priority. Gistly is built with enterprise-grade
  security.
</Info>

- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions and SSO support
- **Data Residency**: Choose your data storage region
- **Compliance**: SOC 2 Type II (in progress)
