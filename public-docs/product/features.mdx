# Features

Discover everything Gistly can do to help you get more value from your meetings.

## Core Features

### Automatic Transcription

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Accuracy" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Our advanced speech-to-text models deliver 95%+ accuracy for clear audio,
    with support for: - Multiple speakers with diarization - Technical
    terminology - Multiple languages and accents
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Smart Formatting" icon="text">
    Transcripts are automatically formatted with: - Speaker labels - Timestamps
    - Paragraph breaks - Punctuation and capitalization
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Meeting Insights

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI Summaries" icon="file-lines">
    Get concise meeting summaries highlighting: - Key discussion points -
    Decisions made - Action items assigned - Follow-up requirements
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Topic Extraction" icon="tags">
    Automatically identify and categorize topics discussed in your meetings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sentiment Analysis" icon="face-smile">
    Understand the tone and sentiment of conversations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Analytics Dashboard

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Meeting Metrics" icon="chart-bar">
    Track key metrics including: - Talk time distribution - Question frequency -
    Participation rates - Meeting duration trends
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team Performance" icon="users">
    Compare performance across team members and identify coaching opportunities.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom Reports" icon="file-chart-pie">
    Generate reports for stakeholders with customizable metrics and time
    periods.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security & Compliance

<Info>
  Your data security is our top priority. Gistly is built with enterprise-grade
  security.
</Info>

- **Encryption**: Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions and SSO support
- **Data Residency**: Choose your data storage region
- **Compliance**: SOC 2 Type II (in progress)
