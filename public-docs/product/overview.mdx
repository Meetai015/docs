# What is Gistly?

Gistly is an AI-powered meeting analytics platform that automatically transforms your meeting recordings into actionable insights, transcripts, and reports.

## How It Works

<Steps>

## Who Uses Gistly?

- **Sales Teams** - Track call performance, coach reps, and improve win rates
- **Customer Success** - Monitor customer health and identify risks early
- **Product Teams** - Capture user feedback and feature requests
- **Leadership** - Get visibility into team performance and communication patterns
