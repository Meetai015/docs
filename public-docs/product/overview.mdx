# What is Gistly?

Gistly is an AI-powered conversation intelligence platform that helps teams across sales, support, and collections drive better outcomes from every customer interaction. Automatically transcribe, analyze, and score conversations to improve conversions, recovery rates, and CSAT — while keeping compliance in check.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Platform">
    Integrate with your telephony, CRM, or video conferencing platform. Gistly supports VoIP systems, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Processing">
    Conversations are automatically transcribed, summarized, and analyzed within minutes of completion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Quality Scoring">
    Every call is scored against your custom templates — covering opening, objection handling, compliance, closing, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Actionable Insights">
    Get dashboards, red flag alerts, coaching recommendations, and detailed reports to drive team performance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use Cases

- **Sales & Admissions** — Track call performance, coach reps, and improve conversion rates
- **Customer Support** — Monitor CSAT, ensure SOP compliance, and reduce resolution time
- **Collections** — Improve recovery rates while maintaining regulatory compliance
- **Voice AI Monitoring** — Observe and optimize AI agent conversations at scale
