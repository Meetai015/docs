# Quickstart

Get up and running with Gistly Call Analytics quickly.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [console.gistly.ai](https://console.gistly.ai)
2. Sign up with your work email or Google account
3. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Set Up Your Organization

1. Create a new organization or join an existing one
2. Invite your team members
3. Configure your organization settings

## Step 3: Upload Your First Meeting

1. Navigate to **Meetings** in the sidebar
2. Click **Upload Meeting**
3. Select your audio or video file
4. Wait for processing to complete (typically 5-10 minutes)

**Supported Formats:**
- Audio: mp3, wav, aac, m4a, ogg, flac
- Video: mp4, mkv, avi, mov, wmv, flv, webm

## Step 4: View Your Analytics

Once processing is complete, you'll see:
- **Transcript** — Full meeting transcription
- **Summary** — AI-generated meeting summary
- **Quality Scores** — Conversation quality metrics
- **Action Items** — Extracted next steps
